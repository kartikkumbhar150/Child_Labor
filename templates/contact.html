Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a child labor case?

You can report a child labor case through our "Report Case" section on the website. Fill out the form with as much detail as possible, and our team will follow up promptly. You can also call our helpline at 1800 123 4567.

How can I volunteer with ChildSafe?

We welcome volunteers who are passionate about ending child labor. Please send us a message through our contact form specifying your interest in volunteering, and our team will get in touch with you about current opportunities.

How are my donations used?

Your donations directly support our rescue operations, rehabilitation programs, education initiatives, and awareness campaigns. We ensure transparency in our financial reports, which are available on request.

Does ChildSafe partner with other organizations?

Yes, we collaborate with various NGOs, government agencies, and international organizations to maximize our impact. If you represent an organization interested in partnership, please contact us through the form above.

About Us

ChildSafe is dedicated to the elimination of child labor through awareness, advocacy, and action. We work with communities, governments, and organizations to create lasting change.
